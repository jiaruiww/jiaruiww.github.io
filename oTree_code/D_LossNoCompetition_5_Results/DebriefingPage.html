Debriefing

Thank you for participating in this study. The study examined how competitive environments affect happiness and social attitudes. To preserve the validity of the research, some information was temporarily withheld:

Financial Loss Was Simulated：In the quiz, you were told that you might lose part of your payment based on your performance. However, this deduction was only temporary. All participants will receive the full base payment regardless of performance. Any bonuses you earned will also be added. This approach was necessary to comply with Prolific’s payment policy and to preserve the integrity of the study.

This minor and non-harmful hidden information was used to prevent bias and ensure valid research results. The study followed ethical guidelines, and no harm was caused. If you do not want your data used now that you know the full purpose, email us at jiaruiw@bu.edu with your Prolific ID.

Thank you again for your time and contribution!
